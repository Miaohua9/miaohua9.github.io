title: "A Multi-Agent Smart Travel Planner"
excerpt: "Planning a trip often means jumping between maps, weather apps, hotel platforms, restaurant guides, and travel blogs.
This makes the process slow, fragmented, and prone to inconsistent information. Multi-Agent Smart Travel Planner was built to 
simplify that workflow by coordinating specialized AI agents that collect, reason over, and organize travel information in one place. 
This project is a full-stack multi-agent travel planning system built with a Vue 3 frontend and a FastAPI/Pydantic backend. 
Users provide travel preferences through a planning form, and the backend coordinates multiple role-based agents to generate 
personalized itineraries with attractions, weather context, hotel options, restaurant recommendations, 
and map-based visualization. <br/><img src='/images/Smart_travel.png' style='margin-top: 15px;'>"
