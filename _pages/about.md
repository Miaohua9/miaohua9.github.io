Welcome to my homepage!
I am an applied Data Scientist and Machine Learning researcher with over 10 years of experience delivering end-to-end AI and machine learning solutions in real-world settings, from problem framing and data analysis through model development, evaluation, deployment, and monitoring. I have strong background in predictive modelling, statistical analysis, optimisation, and robust learning under limited, unbalanced, noisy, and uncertain data conditions, supported by deep quantitative foundations in regression, compressed sensing, and convex/non-convex optimisation. 

My work has translated advanced AI research into practical impact across marine monitoring, satellite remote sensing, digital health, agriculture, autonomous navigation, biometric security, industry quality control. Recent focus includes Generative AI, conditional diffusion models, vision-language models, foundation models, and LLM-based systems including RAG and agentic workflows. I am particularly interested in building data-driven solutions that combine technical depth with real-world value. Author of 30+ publications in leading journals and top-tier conferences.
